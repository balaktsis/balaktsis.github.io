About Me

I'm a PhD candidate at Aristotle University of Thessaloniki (AUTh), specializing in Big Data management, Process Science, and Pattern Mining. I work as a Data Engineer and Researcher at Datalab - Data and Web Science Lab, at School of Informatics of AUTh.
